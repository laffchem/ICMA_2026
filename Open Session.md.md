### Starting Points

Talk with individuals using AI, understand what processes they are using it for.

Problem Statements using open ended questions may result in some unexpected insight.

Five questions to build a problem statement. -- *Good example*
- What is the specific operational problem?
- What does it cost today?
- What would measurably better look like?
- What constraints exist?
- What must a 90-day proof of concept show?
