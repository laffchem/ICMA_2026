# Humanity in the Age of AI & Social Media
*Local Gov got caught off guard with social media, and AI is here so pairing these topics is good to prepare municipalities*

## Value Shifts
- What value do employees bring that will change through the use of AI?
  - Example: Permitting being automated could fast track issuing a permit, but the value shift for the technician would be more on a customer service focus and guidance focus.
- AI can recommend decisions, but can it discern?
  - Data informs, discernment humanizes

## As AI absorbs technical production, humans must become exceptional at meaning-making
- Competitive advantage of public institutions in an AI era.
- AI is leveling the playing field between expertise due to being able to analyze complexities formerly passed to expertise
- Communications departments should be communicating successes on social media as most people will not see the work and value the city provides to citizens

## New Communications Landscape
- Digital Media is the current main form of media consumption except for the 55+ demographic.
- The primary media consumption for all other age groups are Social Media / Video Networks
- Social Media has a direct comparison with website consumption depending on age groups
  - Youtube has the largest platform of all available platforms
- 60% of Americans are Millenials or Younger
  - Average age of users
  - |Service|Age Group|
    |-------|---------|
    |Facebook|55+|
    |Instagram|Millenials & Younger|
    |TikTok|Millenials & Younger|
    |Youtube|All Age Groups|
  - This means the city needs to communicate with the community differently. Email newsletters and facebook posts only reach a fraction of the community.

## Social Media Tool Kit for managers
1. Meet your audience where they are.
2. Video first (no more flyers)
3. Efficiency tools make it easier than ever
4. A culture of content
5. Permission + Structure is how you solve content creation
  a. Content Based not Output Based

**65% of all content being consumed is video.**
## Meeting Your Audience Where They Are
**This cannot just be a communications person gatekeeping**
- They need to be a strategist and allow users to make their own content and review it
- If they are too protective it won't work, because you'll only ever have 1 perspective and seem less genuine to the community
- Facebook content should only be 3-5 posts / week.
- No cross-posting from facebook to instagram.
- Focus on making video content for Instagram.
  - < 60 second reels (2-4x / week)
  - < 20 second stories (daily!)
- Create engaging content to help form a parasocial connection between the Team Town & Neighbors they serve
- Engaging, human stories are important
- Show your work to the community!
